MineCoin

MineCoin - is a decentralized cryptocurrency the emission of which is realized by means of mining and which is meant to be the main element of decentralized financial system built on block-chain technology. Mincoin is a resource that ensures the functioning of the Minex platform. With its help you can purchase digital company stocks, emission of which is occurred of the Minex platform. Minecoin is also a tool to determine the value of the owner vote in assets management posted on Minex.

// One of the main features of Minex is avoiding intermediaries for both part of investment

Minex platform

Minex platform - CBMI (Crowdbusiness management and investing) an open source platform for attracting investment and business management. The platform allows you to discuss the business perspectives, to take part in the management of financial resources, to provide the control over the investment object, receive dividends based on the results of the project, to trade cryptocurrency at the decentralized exchange market integrated into platform.

On the subscription stage the investors are suggested to gain the minecoins including bonus of “primary participant”. The MNC price on the subscription stage starts from 1000mnc per 1 BTC and rises up to 20% after the sale of every 500,000 coins. Total subscription offered – 2.000.000 MNC
